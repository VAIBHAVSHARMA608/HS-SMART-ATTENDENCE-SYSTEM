Welcome, Teacher!

Use the menu to mark attendance, view students, add remarks, and generate reports.
Note: All data will be managed securely using Node.js and MongoDB in the backend.

Menu Guide:
  • Dashboard: View your classes, quick stats, and notifications.
  • Mark Attendance: Take daily attendance for your assigned classes.
  • Students List: View, search, and manage student details.
  • Reports & Remarks: Add remarks, generate reports, and communicate with parents.
