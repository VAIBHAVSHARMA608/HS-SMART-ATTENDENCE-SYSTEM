Welcome to HR Dashboard

Use the menu to manage employees, attendance, salary, and reports.
Note: All data will be managed securely using Node.js and MongoDB in the backend.

Menu Guide:
  • Dashboard: HR overview, quick stats, and notifications.
  • Employees: View, add, and manage employee records.
  • Attendance: Monitor and manage staff attendance.
  • Salary: Process payroll and view salary details.
  • Reports: Generate HR and compliance reports.
